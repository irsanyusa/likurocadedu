After a thrilling first two seasons, the soccer underdog docuseries “Welcome to Wrexham,” which stars Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney, is back for Season 3.

The third episode of Season 3 “Notts Again” drops on Thursday, May 9. But, if you want to catch up with the first two episodes of Season 3, as well as all of Season 1 and 2, FX’s “Welcome to Wrexham” is streamable on Hulu for subscribers only.

watch ‘Welcome to Wrexham’ on Hulu

At the moment, Hulu has a 30-day free trial for new subscribers to check out the streaming service before committing to another month. Afterwards, you can either cancel it, or keep watching Hulu starting at $7.99 per month for the ad-supported plan. However, to go ad-free, the price jumps up to $17.99 per month.

watch ‘Welcome to Wrexham’ on Hulu

The docuseries follows Reynolds and McElhenney acquiring ownership of the soccer club Wrexham A.F.C. and the Racecourse Ground stadium, which are based in Wales. Although the team is in the minor league EFL League Two, Wrexham A.F.C. works their way to win entry into the higher-tier EFL League One — all in the hopes to make it into the EFL Championship League one day.
