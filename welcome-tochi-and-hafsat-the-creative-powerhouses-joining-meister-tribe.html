Welcome Tochi and Hafsat: The creative powerhouses joining Meister Tribe!

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  601 words  |  Aldo Pusey

That is why we are beyond excited to welcome two extraordinary individuals into the Meisters Tribe.

Wondering what the ‘‘Meister Tribe’’ means? It is a group of individuals who are creatives, music artists, and fashionistas known for their original, unconventional and unique artistry.

Want to know if our new Meisters are anything like what we just described above? Well, come meet them! We bet you can’t contain your curiosity.

Tochi: Not Your Regular Hair Stylist

If you haven't heard of Tochi Nwosu, you don’t know ball! Tochi who has been styling hair professionally since his second year in the university has gone ahead to build for himself a global brand. This Meister, who could not be overlooked as a result of his top-notch creativity in the hair industry founded Tochi Mint Stylez - a popular hair brand which has birthed several salons in different countries. It’s no wonder he is known as the number one loctician in Nigeria, and the go-to professional loctician for many celebrities - talk of utmost dedication and being a master of one’s craft!

That’s not all! Asides being an amazing hairstylist, Tochi is also committed to employing skilled hairstylists, encouraging and empowering other people to become masters of their own craft. We are inspired and excited to see how he would channel his creativity and make innovative contributions to the Meister Tribe.

Meet Hafsat: The Exceptional Wordsmith

ADVERTISEMENT

Multiple award-winning spoken word poet, member of the Grammy recording academy, Actor, Voice actor, writer, and fashion enthusiast - Hafsat sure does wear many caps and they all fit well! With her words which have the power to move mountains, this phenomenal human has refused to back down in using her words as a tool to address critical social issues! This is going great for her as she is already making waves, having won several awards, including the prestigious World Bank YouthActonEdu award. We wish (or not) that was all, but talk about a jack of all trades, and master of every single one, Hafsat also owns a fashion brand and her fashion inspos sure have a hand in making many youths look stunning while expressing their true selves through fashion! We can’t wait to see how she will inspire young adults to embrace their unique voices and creativity.

Why Tochi and Hafsat?

Tochi and Hafsat represent the wider range of young adults who are keen on pursuing their passions without fear. Hence, through this new addition to the Meister Tribe, Jägermeister reinforces its commitment to inspiring young adults in pursuit of their passions with authenticity and without fear. These newest Meisters are set to join other ambassadors in the Meisters Tribe to inspire unconventional thinkers and doers alike.

What’s More?

The excitement doesn't stop there! Jägermeister Nigeria is also celebrating the entire Meisters Tribe. This is a perfect opportunity for us to recognise all our incredible Meisters - Denrele, Small Doctor, Whitemoney, Commissioner DJ Wysei, Big Bimi, Tevris, Orange Nerd, Femi, and Dare, and their achievements.

The best part of this is that you also can be a part of the Meisters Tribe! Yup! You read that right! All you need to do is get yourself a bottle of Jägermeister drink, and make it your companion as you dedicate yourself to becoming a master of your own craft. It's all about creating a community that thrives on creativity, independence, and self-expression, after all. So cheers to your journey to becoming a Meister as well!
