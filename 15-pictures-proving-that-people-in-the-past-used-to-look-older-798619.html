15 Pictures Proving That People in the Past Used to Look Older

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  81 words  |  Aldo Pusey

It all started when a former baseball player raised a curious question on Twitter. “This is nonsense,” he wrote, “but did people in the past look older when they were younger?” What he really meant was that people used to age much faster than they do today. The replies on the thread very clearly showed that the answer to this question was “yes.” Twitter became filled with images of people who, though in their early thirties, looked like they were in their fifties, and of celebrities, who many years ago, looked older than they are now.
