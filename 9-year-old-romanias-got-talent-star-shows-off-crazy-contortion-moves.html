On Romania’s Got Talent 2020, a 9-year-old girl wowed the judges with her incredible contortion moves. Her name is Bianca Purice, and she put on an amazing audition.

What’s even more impressive is that Bianca kept a smile on her face throughout the performance.

9-Year-Old Contortionist Auditions For ‘Romania’s Got Talent’

Bianca showed off some seriously cool contortion moves like it was nothing. She started off by pulling one of her legs over her head and rolling across the stage. After that, she planted her feet in front of her face and smiled at the judges.

WATCH ACT COMBINE TANGO AND POLE DANCING LIKE YOU’VE NEVER SEEN BEFORE ON ‘ROMANIA’S GOT TALENT’

As the performance continued, Bianca performed back-bending moves with seemingly no effort at all, going along with the music as it became more and more dramatic. She also incorporated dance and gymnastics into the routine, performing flips and spins across the stage.

The judges were visibly impressed by the audition. Three of them even gave her a standing ovation, along with the Romania’s Got Talent audience. Bianca blew them a kiss as a thank-you for their praise over the performance.

Bianca Returned to the Show in 2021

Bianca, now 10 years old, returned to Romania’s Got Talent earlier this year. Instead of her pink and white outfit from the first audition, Bianca wore a snakeskin costume, and seemed to emulate a snake in her routine.

‘ASIA’S GOT TALENT’ CONTORTIONIST SHOOTS AN ARROW WITH HER FEET

Bianca’s second Got Talent performance was even more elaborate than the first. She showed off her incredible balance in addition to her talent for contortion. This audition was also more fast-paced, as she spun and flipped her way across the stage, turning it into even more of a dance routine.

The young contortionist has appeared on other television programs, including Next Star in 2018. During that performance, Bianca showed off her moves while spinning inside a metal globe. There’s no denying she has a bright future ahead of her.
