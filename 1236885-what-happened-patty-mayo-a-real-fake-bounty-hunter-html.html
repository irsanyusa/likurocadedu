Also, the actor usually wears a deputy sheriff uniform, uses a vehicle fitted with emergency lights and sirens, and has a handgun and a taser props. His incredible acting skills make most of his viewers wonder why the YouTuber has never considered being an actual bounty hunter.

Is Patty Mayo a real cop?

According to the Oregon State Sheriffs' Association, Mayo is not a real bounty hunter, and his deputy sheriff uniform is a costume. The association's statement about Mayo reads:

We are aware of the YouTuber Patty Mayo and his activities in Deschutes County. Mr Mayo is not a member of law enforcement nor affiliated in any way with any Sheriff's Office in Oregon. He is an actor, and the uniform he wears is a costume. Additionally, everyone in his videos is part of his production crew or paid actors/actresses.

Is Patty Mayo actually a sheriff?

The YouTube personality is not a sheriff. While he shoots his videos in Deschutes County, Oregon, USA, he is not a representative of any law enforcement agency in Deschutes or any other county in the United States.

In his videos, Mayo and his crew act as deputies from a sheriff's office known as DBSO. The DBSO is not an actual law enforcement officer. Mayo created a fictional sheriff's office for his YouTube content. In the United States, a sheriff is a county's elected law enforcement official. Mayo is neither an elected sheriff nor is he a licensed bounty hunter.
