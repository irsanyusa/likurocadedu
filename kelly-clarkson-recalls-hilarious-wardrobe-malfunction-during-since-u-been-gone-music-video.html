We all have our embarrassing moments, and it turns out that even celebrities like Kelly Clarkson are not immune to them. The popular singer and talk show host recently shared a funny story about a wardrobe malfunction that happened during the filming of her “Since U Been Gone” music video.

Kelly Clarkson Was “Commando” While Filming “Since U Been Gone” Music Video

During an episode of The Kelly Clarkson Show, Clarkson had a candid conversation with guest Kyle Richards about their Halloween outfits. Kyle, dressed in a snug leather catsuit, humorously described it as feeling like “one big Spanx from ankle to neck” and shared her fear of any wardrobe mishaps. That’s when Clarkson chimed in with her own wardrobe malfunction story.

She explained that during the filming of the music video, the director asked her to squat down. The problem was that she was wearing extremely tight leather pants at the time.

“He was like, ‘Go down and pop back up!’ I was like, ‘Oh, okay, cool.’ And I went down and just, ‘Whoops!'” Clarkson said. “

What made the situation even more awkward was the fact that Clarkson was going “commando” underneath her pants. In the moment, she turned to the drummer behind her and humorously commented, “Oh sh*t, that’s a lot of moon, man. I’m sorry.”

This isn’t the first time Clarkson has openly discussed wardrobe malfunctions, especially ones involving a lack of underwear. In a previous episode of her talk show, she shared a different mishap where her dress almost came off during a performance because the zipper wasn’t secure. She laughed about the situation and said, “That would have gone real European beach real quick.”

Kelly Clarkson Can Handle Unexpected Situations Really Well

Clarkson has a history of handling such situations with humor and grace. In a recent incident where she accidentally exposed her chest while performing, she playfully acknowledged the issue and continued the show with a lighthearted attitude, making her audience laugh and feel at ease.

One of the many reasons why fans adore Clarkson is her charming ability to share her real-life, relatable experiences. Her candid and down-to-earth nature makes her an approachable and endearing figure in the world of music and entertainment.

In addition to her delightful anecdotes, Clarkson continues to entertain her viewers with her hit songs and engaging segments on The Kelly Clarkson Show. She embraces various themes and costumes, as seen in her Halloween episode where she dressed as a “Rock and Roll Vampire.” This season, she moved her show from Los Angeles to New York, seeking a fresh start and a new chapter in her life following a painful divorce.
