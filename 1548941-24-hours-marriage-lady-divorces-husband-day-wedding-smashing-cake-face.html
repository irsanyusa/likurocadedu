A lady divorced her husband barely 24 hours after they got wedded because the man smashed cake on her face.

The story was shared on Twitter by Hibzster, and it has gone viral, raking in over 7 million views.

The bride had requested her husband not to rub cake on her face during their wedding as she did not like it.

After the wedding, the woman told her husband it was over between them because he broke the rule.
