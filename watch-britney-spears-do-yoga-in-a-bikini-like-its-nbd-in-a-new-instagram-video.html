Watch Britney Spears Do Yoga In A Bikini Like It's NBD In A New Instagram Video

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  225 words  |  Martina Birk

In the video, the "Make Me" singer can be seen wearing a teal bikini while doing yoga on top of a partner, who appears to be her boyfriend, personal trainer Sam Asghari. She does a couple of back bends followed by a few inversions while her partner holds her up. Brit doesnt stop there, though: She also raises her legs, spreads them, and does stuff in the air that most people cant even do when theyre on solid land.

"Only in LA can you wear a bathing suit in October! Its so beautiful out .... and first time to walk on my hands up and down stairs," she wrote in the caption . Check out the acrobatic action here:

Its no secret that Britney is flexible: She regularly shares videos on Instagram of herself doing handstands, back bends, and splits. Shes also super into yoga and, apparently, bikini yoga.

Back in May, she shared an Instagram video of herself doing yoga on what appeared to be a golf course set to the tune of Cardi Bs "I Like It." In that vid, Britney did more back bends and even walked on her handsya know, as one does. "Yoga on the green. I really like it LIKE THAT !!" she wrote on the caption.

So yeah, you might say Britneys pretty good at yoga...
