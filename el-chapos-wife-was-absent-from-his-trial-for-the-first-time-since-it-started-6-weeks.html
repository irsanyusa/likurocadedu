El Chapo's wife was absent from his trial for the first time since it started 6 weeks ago

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  157 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Until Monday, Aispuro had attended every day of Guzman's federal trial in Brooklyn.

A source close to Aispuro told The Post that the 29-year-old was still in New York, but it was unclear exactly where she was or why she missed court.

The former beauty queen's absence came just days after she gave a rare public interview to Telemundo , in which she defended her husband and called him a "normal, ordinary person."

The American-born mother-of-two told Telemundo that she had never seen her husband doing anything illegal, according to translations from The New York Post .

"[The media] made him too famous," Aispuro said of her 61-year-old husband, whom she married on her 18th birthday in 2007. "It's not fair."

After years on the run, Guzman was extradited to the United States last year and was held in solitary confinement in a high-security prison as he awaited trial, according to the Associated Press.
