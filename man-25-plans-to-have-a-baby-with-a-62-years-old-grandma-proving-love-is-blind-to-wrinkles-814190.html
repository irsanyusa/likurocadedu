Man, 25, Plans to Have a Baby With a 62-Years-Old Grandma, Proving Love Is Blind to Wrinkles

 Posted on May 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  414 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Cheryl McGregor, 62, found love with Quran McCain, 25, despite their age difference. They believe they’ve discovered their perfect match and are happily married. McGregor, a grandmother of 17, and McCain are determined to have children together, despite any judgment from others.

Cheryl McGregor and Quran McCain crossed paths when Cheryl’s son introduced them.

Quran McCain and Cheryl McGregor’s love story started when they initially met at a restaurant where McCain worked under McGregor’s son. However, they didn’t have much interaction at that time. Years later, they unexpectedly crossed paths again at a store where McGregor worked as a cashier, and that’s when they decided to reconnect and stay in touch.

Age and a significant 37-year gap couldn’t stop them from falling in love.

They started as friends but soon recognized a mutual romantic attraction and began dating. At the time, McGregor was 60, a mother of 7, and a grandmother of 17. Despite being just 23 years old, McCain felt a deep spiritual connection with McGregor. After a single date, he was certain of his feelings for her.

After a year of dating, they got engaged and happily married in 2021.

Within just a year of dating, the couple took a significant step forward and got engaged. They also exchanged vows and married during that same year. However, their journey was not without challenges, as they had to confront obstacles such as disapproval from McGregor’s family. Nevertheless, their commitment to one another remained unwavering.

“Nobody was accepting, everyone thought we were joking or thought it was all games,” McCain revealed about his relationship with McGregor. On the other hand, McCain’s family quickly grew to love his bride and soon accepted her.

They went viral on TikTok after sharing videos a few years ago.

McGregor and McCain gained online fame through their viral TikTok videos posted a couple of years ago. Their popular clips often showcase their dance moves or address questions and criticism about their relationship. Despite facing occasional negativity, McGregor’s TikTok account has amassed more than 4 million followers.

Despite facing hateful comments, their love continues to flourish and strengthen with each passing day.

Despite criticism from family and strangers, McGregor and McCain’s bond has only grown stronger, defying all odds. “It’s different, it’s amazing, he’s very compassionate to me, and I think it’s because he’s a younger man,” McGregor gushed about her husband. “There’s just something between him and me that I never thought we would be together like this, he’s just an amazing person,” she added.

Their shared aspiration is to build a family together, which remains their greatest dream.

McGregor and McCain’s shared goal is to start a family, despite facing criticism and ridicule from their followers due to the age difference and McGregor’s age. They are determined to make their dream a reality by exploring the option of hiring a surrogate to help them welcome a baby into their lives.
